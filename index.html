Your Todo List v4.2

Track your todos

1-ADD
Type in what you gotta do in the input field & press enter or tap the '+' button to add a new todo.
2-SEE
After you add a todo, you'll see it below right away. Your latest items will appear first on the list.
3-CHECK
Just tap the dark blue checkbox on the left of your todo to check off what you've done. The checkbox will turn light blue & your todo will be crossed out. If you've finished all your todos, you can easily tap the dark blue checkbox with the checkmark inside it to toggle all of your completed todos.
4-CHANGE
Just tap your todo item & you'll be able to make changes. After you make your change, you can either press enter key on your keyboard or just tap outside of the box & it will save your changes. If you don't want to save your change, you can press escape key on your keyword & it will go back to what you had before.
5-DELETE
Just tap the 'x' button on the right side of your todo item to delete it right away.
